Illegal DDoS Attacks

Warning: DDoS attacks are illegal and can result in serious consequences.

A DDoS attack, or distributed denial-of-service attack, is a malicious attempt to disrupt the normal traffic of a targeted server or network by overwhelming it with a flood of internet traffic from multiple sources.

These attacks are legal because they can cause serious damage to businesses, organizations, and individuals. They can lead to loss of revenue, damage to reputation, and even data breaches.

If you suspect that you are a victim of a DDoS attack, it is important to report it to the proper authorities and seek assistance from a cybersecurity professional.
