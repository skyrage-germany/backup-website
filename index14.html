Minecraft Facts

Did you know that Minecraft was created by Markus "Notch" Persson in 2009?

Over 91 million people play Minecraft every month.

Minecraft was originally called "Cave Game" and was inspired by games like Infiniminer and Dwarf Fortress.

The Ender Dragon is the final boss in Minecraft and was added in version 1.0.0.

You can create your own custom skins for your Minecraft character.

Minecraft has won numerous awards, including the BAFTA Games Award for Best British Game in 2012.

Minecraft has its own official festival, called Minecon, which is held annually.

There are multiple versions of Minecraft, including Minecraft: Java Edition, Minecraft: Bedrock Edition, and Minecraft: Education Edition.

The default Minecraft texture pack is called "Programmer Art" and was added in version 1.14.

Minecraft has its own merchandise, including toys, clothing, and accessories.

For more Minecraft facts, check out The Official Minecraft Wiki.
